The beginning of CyberSmithSECURE

Leading the Charge

Shaping Cybersecurity Excellence

CyberSmithSECURE is a boutique cybersecurity firm that stands out for its exceptional expertise in safeguarding businesses from traditional and emerging cyber threats. Founded by the visionary Smith Gonsalves when he was just 20 years old, CyberSmithSECURE is now in its fourth year of operation, and has assembled a formidable team of industry experts, including seasoned veterans with decades of experience and prestigious certifications such as OSCP, CISA, CISSP, Cert-In and other specialized credentials in ethical auditing.

Headquartered in India, CyberSmithSECURE has strategically positioned regional offices in key global hubs, including the UAE, UK, Canada, and the United States. We are proud to be a registered entity with Startup India, emphasizing our commitment to innovation and growth in the cybersecurity sector. Furthermore, CyberSmithSECURE is aligned with industry standards, adhering to ISO 27002:2022, ISO 27001:2013 and ISO 9001:2015, ensuring the highest levels of quality and security in our services. CyberSmithSECURE proudly holds the distinction of being registered as a startup with India's Ministry of Commerce and Industry since 2009-2015, in addition to its registration as a Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME) organization.

Cyber Resilience

As cybersecurity professionals, we believe in ethical hacking to strengthen your defenses. We adhere to strict ethical guidelines and use our hacking skills for the benefit of our clients, identifying vulnerabilities before malicious actors can exploit them.
